Update: Stockfish 13 for arm64-v8a and armeabi-v7a processors.
Italian translation by Emanuele Formica.

This package provides the following open source engines:

Stockfish 11

Stockfish 12

Stockfish 13

Stockfish 20200926

Stockfish 20200926 NNUE

To use these engines, you need a chess application that is compatible with the Open Exchange protocol.
Recommended chess GUIs:

* Chess for All
* DroidFish
* Scid on the go
* Chess PGN Master
* Chess for Android
* Chess (Jeroen Carolus)
* Acid Ape Chess

Stockfish is developed by Tord Romstad, Marco Costalba, Gary Linscott and Joona Kiiiski.
The engines are compiled by Peter Österlund and Bernhard C. März.
